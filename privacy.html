Privacy Policy

How verification information is handled.

Information Entered

Users may enter a trade licence number to request a verification result. This information is used only to process the verification request and display the result.

Data Protection

The service is designed to handle searches securely and to protect the integrity of verification records. Personal contact details are not requested through the verification form.

Email Communication

If you contact support by email, the information you provide will be used to respond to your request and assist with verification-related issues.

Updates

This policy may be updated as the service changes. Continued use of the site means you accept the current version of this policy.
